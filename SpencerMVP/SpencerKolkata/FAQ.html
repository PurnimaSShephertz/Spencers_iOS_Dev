FAQs

How do I place my first online order?

Open your app and register with your email id and password. If you are an existing user of spencer.in log in to the app with your user id and password.

Do you take orders on call?

We do not take orders on call currently, please log on to our website/app to shop

Are there any charges for registration?

No, there are no registration charges

Can I have multiple registrations?

No. Please use a single account to keep track of your purchases & loyalty points

Do you service my area?

Once you access the app, it requests for detecting your location. If you are in an area not serviced by the delivery team, you will get regret message mentioning the same. You may then go ahead and manually select you area of delivery by choosing from the drop down list which is created once you start typing out your area of delivery.

Please ensure that the area entered manually/automatically picked up by the app should be same as the delivery area of your order. In case there is mismatch, please choose the area where you want delivery of your order while entering the app.

How do I search for a product?

You can search for a product by typing out the product name in the ‘Search’ ribbon. Once you start writing the product name in the search ribbon, a drop down is created. You may choose from the drop down in case you find your preferred product else press search on the keypad to view a larger list for your search criteria

Is there a Minimum Order Value?

Yes. At present the minimum order value is R 500

Can I amend my order?

You cannot amend the order once the payment is processed. In such a scenario, you can cancel the order and then again place a new order.

While the order is in the cart you can either change the quantity or remove the product altogether.

How do I pay for my purchases?

We accept Cash on Delivery through cash or through Credit/Debit Card, Internet banking, e-Wallets

Is it safe to use my Credit Card/Debit Card online?

Your online transaction on Spencers.in is secure with the highest levels of transaction security currently available on the Internet. We use 256-bit encryption technology to protect your card information while securely transmitting it to the respective banks for payment processing.
All credit card and debit card payments on the app are processed through secure and trusted payment gateways managed by leading banks. Banks now use the 3D Secure password service for online transactions, providing an additional layer of security through identity verification.

We realize the importance of a strong fraud detection and resolution capability. We and our online payments partners monitor transactions continuously for suspicious activity and flag potentially fraudulent transactions for manual verification by our team. 

In the rarest of rare cases, when our team is unable to rule out the possibility of fraud categorically, the transaction is kept on hold, and the customer is requested to provide identity documents. The ID documents help us ensure that the purchases were indeed made by a genuine card holder. We apologize for any inconvenience that may be caused to customers and request them to bear with us in the larger interest of ensuring a safe and secure environment for online transactions

How do I use my Credit Card/Debit Card Online?

Enter your Card Number & Expiry Date (these details are on the front side of your card) The CVV number is a 3 digit code that you will find on the flip side of your card. Simply click on ‘Make Payment’ to complete your transaction.
You will have to validate your card details via an OTP sent to the mobile number registered with your bank or by entering the grid numbers provided on your debit card

How do I use Net Banking?

Choose your Banking Partner from the drop down list provided, the page will redirect to your bank’s internet banking site. Login, using your banking user ID & password.
You may be asked to validate your transaction using the grid numbers on your debit card or an OTP sent to the mobile number registered with your bank

Are there any delivery charges?

There are no delivery charges

Can I change my delivery slot?

Unfortunately, you cannot change your delivery slot after placing your orders

Do you deliver on Sundays & Public Holidays?

Yes, we deliver on all weekends & Public Holidays, On Holi – We will not offer any delivery slots in the morning & on Diwali, we will not offer any delivery slots in the evening

How are Fruits, Vegetables, Fish and Meat; picked, packed & delivered?

Fruits, Vegetables, Fish & Meat undergo a stringent Quality Check to ensure that only the right quality is picked & packed – Fruits & Vegetables are weighed on our scales, For Fish & Meat, weights are assigned only to the cleaned product. Chill pads & insulation boxes are used for delivery to maintain freshness

Can I cancel my order?

Orders can be cancelled two hours before the start of your chosen delivery slot. You will have to reach our call center and place a request. Our call center number is 1800-2660-134. Example: If your chosen slot is 9:30AM – 11:30AM, you can cancel your order by calling the call center up to 7:30AM

I have received my order in a damaged/broken state, what do I do?

Our stringent Quality Check process ensures that we do not ship any item that falls short of your expectations, if this event arises, we follow a ‘No Questions Asked’ Refusal Policy at your doorstep. Value of the goods refused, will be credited to the source of payment in case of all pre-paid orders.

How can I redeem ‘My Wallet’ points?

While checking out check the spencer’s wallet box to utilize your spencer’s wallet balance while paying for the order. Wallet points have a validity of 30 days.

I need to return an item, can I arrange for it to be picked up?

No. Reverse pickup of returned items is not done by our delivery team. All returns are entertained at designated spencer’s store and the product is to be carried by the customer to the store. Please reach out to our call center to locate your store of return.

Our detailed return policy is as below.
  1. Customers can check for the store where they can return their purchased goods by reaching out to customer care
  2. All returns will be accepted at the store from where the order was delivered
  3. Customers arriving at the store will be guided to the CSD desk of the Hyper stores to initiate the return process
  4. Return of goods is to be accepted basis the return criteria for various type of goods as mentioned below
    1. No return or exchange of products belonging to Undergarments, Fruits & Vegetables, Fish & Meat, Electronics & Electrical and Chilled & Frozen will be entertained
    2. For packed rice units, in case the packaging is open, no deduction will be made in case of weight loss of less than equal to 5%. In case, the weight loss is more than 5% then proportionate return will be made.
    3. Exchange of products belonging to all other categories will be entertained within seven days from the date of sale
    4. Products on Promotion & offers to be returned in full. Part returns are not allowed. Example If buy 2 get 1 free available on certain products, then entire products to be returned
  5. Exchange shall be entertained for unopened packs only. Unsealed packages will be only considered if case of quality related issues within 48 hours from the time of delivery.
  6. Once return is accepted, the customer spencer’s wallet will be credited with money equivalent to the value of the returned products. No refund of cash will be entertained for returns.
  7. Money transferred to spencer’s wallet in lieu of returned goods will be valid for 30 days
How much time does it take to process refunds?

Refunds will be credited within 2 working days. Money will be credited to the original source of payment for refusal to accept whole/part of order or for non-fulfillment of whole/part of order. Money will be credited to spencer’s wallet in case a part or whole of the order returned after accepting delivery.
