About Us

Spencer’s Retail, a part of RP – Sanjiv Goenka Group is one of India’s first organized retailers operating primarily in food and grocery segment. Spencer’s is also a pioneer in Omni- channel retailing in the country.

It offers a wide range and assortment of products in food such as fresh fruits & vegetables, staples, personal care & beauty, FMCG, organic food, specialty food, fish & meat, cold cuts, dairy, chilled & frozen and other utility products for your home including home linen, tableware, kitchenware and a complete range of apparel and accessories for men, women and kids.

Spencer’s today runs about 120 stores, including 37 large format stores across 35 cities in India employing more than 5000 professionals who are specially trained to meet consumer needs.

Taking a giant leap forward in 2016, Spencer’s retail now brings its core offerings to the doorstep of consumers. Through its online channel, Spencer’s provides grocery shopping convenience from anywhere with a promise to deliver within 3 hours assuring quality products at best prices.

The online version of the business, www.spencers.in is managed by Omnipresent Retail India Pvt Ltd, a wholly owned subsidiary of Spencer’s Retail Limited.

Currently available in Kolkata and NCR, Spencer’s sells over 15000+ products online offering the convenience of delivery at your doorstep. The organization has a footfall of about 3.5 million per month in the physical stores and about 10 lakhs loyal shoppers across the country today.
